About Us

Allow me to introduce myself, My name is Clifton Clayton, I am the proud owner of the Philly Diner in Deland, Florida, which I truly love, but I miss classic Philidelphia diners. I grew up in the restaurant business, and I thought it would be a great idea to bring some classic diner atmosphere and the best of Philly to Deland. At the Philly Diner, our entrees are plentiful, tasty, and served with a a smile. Here at the Philly Diner we love our Philadelphia sports teams. We have the Sunday ticket, so please feel free to join us for all Eagles games -- For every touchdown they score, enjoy a free shot!
